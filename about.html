About the Holigays Project

Holigays Central represents a focused exploration into the "hotel as a destination" concept within the specific niche of LGBTQIA+ exclusive hospitality. This initiative serves as a strategic sandbox environment to understand the dynamics and potential of a segment-specific online travel agency. By concentrating on properties that intentionally cater to the LGBTQIA+ community, Holigays Central aims to aggregate and showcase hotels that prioritize inclusivity, safety, and a deep understanding of this demographic's unique needs and preferences. The project seeks to identify key success factors and operational nuances within this specialized area of the travel industry. The current iteration of Holigays Central is designed to test and refine the "hotel as a destination" model, emphasizing properties that offer comprehensive experiences beyond mere accommodation. This includes hotels with strong on-site amenities, curated events, and a distinct atmosphere that resonates with the LGBTQIA+ community. By focusing on destinations where gay travel is prominent, the platform aims to connect discerning travelers with establishments that genuinely understand and cater to their lifestyles. This targeted approach allows for a deeper understanding of user behavior and the effectiveness of specific marketing strategies within this segment.

Pedro Svaetichin

Pedro Svaetichin, an alumnus of the esteemed Sciences Po in Paris, brings a background rooted in East-European studies to his extensive professional life. His career spans several decades within hospitality sales, reflecting a deep and nuanced understanding of the industry. Possessing fluency in eight European languages, Pedro demonstrates a notable capacity for international engagement and communication across diverse cultures.

A distinctive aspect of Pedro's professional journey includes a significant period of seventeen years dedicated to opera singing and acting. This experience outside the conventional business realm provided him with unique skills in presentation, communication, and connecting with individuals. For the past ten years, Pedro has resided in the United Kingdom, further broadening his European perspective and professional network.

In 2017, Pedro established Arlesiana Ltd, marking his initiative as an entrepreneur. Responding to the circumstances arising during the pandemic, he subsequently founded Holigays Central Ltd. This endeavor was conceived with the dual purpose of creating employment opportunities and offering a clear and secure platform for members of the LGBTQIA+ community during a period of increasing social challenges.

Recognizing the transformative potential of recent advancements in Artificial Intelligence, Pedro made a strategic decision to evolve Holigays Central into a niche-focused Online Travel Agency (OTA). The sophisticated capabilities of AI in managing detailed tagging systems presented a valuable opportunity to effectively connect LGBTQIA+ travelers with precisely tailored accommodation and destination options. This considered adaptation reflects a pragmatic approach to providing a relevant service within a specific market segment.
